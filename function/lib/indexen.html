How do phishing sites steal?

Phishing sites usually disguise themselves as well-known NFT sites. If you click on their inducing button like mint, they'll actually use the ‘approve’ method to authorize your asset to an address and then transfer them away.

How do we protect your crypto assets?

MetaShield can quickly identify approved transactions, check the security status of authorized addresses through blacklist and whitelist, and inform you of the consequence of clicks. It can warn or even block high-risk authorization.
