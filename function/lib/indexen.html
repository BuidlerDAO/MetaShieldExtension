How do phishing sites steal?

Phishing sites usually disguise themselves as well-known NFT sites. If you click on their inducing button like mint, they'll actually use the ‘approve’ or ‘send’ method to authorize your asset or transfer them away.

How do we protect your crypto assets?

MetaShield three-layers protection: Transaction Monitoring, Blacklist and Whitelist Review, Information Display. Traditional security plug-ins have defects like out-of-date updates and incomplete lists, while MetaShield will identify approve and send transactions, detect security and open source status through blacklist and whitelists, fully inform users of transaction information, and trigger necessary warnings and interceptions.
